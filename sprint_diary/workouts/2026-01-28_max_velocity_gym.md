## Gym Session (25-30 min, immediately after track)

### A. Power Expression Contrast (3 rounds, 18 min)

#### A1: Overcoming Isometric Quarter Squat
- **Sets:** 3
- **Duration:** 2×6s max effort
- **Rest:** None (superset into A2)
- **Cue:** Push into immovable bar, recruit maximum motor units
- **Purpose:** Prime CNS for explosive work

#### A2: High Hang Clean Pull (VBT)
- **Sets:** 3×3
- **Progression:** 50kg → 55kg → 60kg
- **Target Velocity:** >2.5 m/s peak velocity
- **Rest:** 2 min after box jumps
- **eNode Tracking:**
  - 50kg: Expect 2.67 m/s
  - 55kg: Expect ~2.60 m/s
  - 60kg: Expect 2.58 m/s
- **Optional 65kg:** ONLY if 60kg maintains >2.55 m/s
- **Stop Rule:** If velocity drops below 2.5 m/s, session ends

#### A3: Box Jump
- **Sets:** 3
- **Reps:** 5 per set
- **Height:** 30-35cm (NOT higher - Ohman rule: max 40cm for sprinters)
- **Focus:** Speed of ground contact, not jump height
- **Target:** Ground contact <160ms
- **Rest:** 2 min before next round
- **Cue:** "Pop off the ground" - elastic response
